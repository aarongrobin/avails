SINGLE PURPOSE DESCRIPTION
===========================
Helps users compose and share their availability across multiple timezones by converting local time slots into formatted schedules for emails and messages.


PERMISSION: storage
===================
Saves user preferences locally including favorite timezones, availability entries, and home timezone setting. Enables settings to persist between sessions. No data is sent to external servers.


PERMISSION: sidePanel
=====================
Displays the extension interface as a Chrome side panel, allowing users to enter availability while browsing without blocking page content.


PRIVACY POLICY
==============
Privacy Policy for Avails

Last updated: January 2026

Data Collection
Avails stores the following data locally in your Chrome browser:
- Your selected home timezone
- Favorite timezone preferences
- Availability entries you type (stored by date)

Data Storage
All data is stored using Chrome's storage API. If signed into Chrome, data may sync across your devices through your Google account. The developer has no access to this data.

Data Sharing
We do not:
- Collect personal information
- Send data to external servers
- Share data with third parties
- Use analytics or tracking

Data Deletion
Clear all data by clicking "Clear Times" in the extension, removing the extension, or clearing Chrome's extension data in Settings.
